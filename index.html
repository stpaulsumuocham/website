About Our Church

Our Contribution to the Diocese

St. Paul's Anglican Church, Umuocham has been a pillar of the Aba Ngwa North Diocese since our establishment. We actively participate in diocesan programs, contribute to missionary work, and support theological education initiatives.

Our church hosts annual diocesan conferences, trains lay readers, and has produced several clergy members serving across the diocese. We take pride in our vibrant choir that represents the diocese at provincial events.

Diocesan Delegates 15+ representatives
Clergy Training 5 ordained priests
